How to control Ryu

  • Hover over Ryu to make him shift to his ready pose
  • When you stop hovering, he stands still again
  • When you click and hold, he throws a hadouken and stays in his throwing stance
  • When you release the click, he returns to his ready pose
  • When you hold "x" he assumes his looking cool pose
  • Release "x" and he returns to his ready pose
